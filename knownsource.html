UX Design, E-Commerce, Website

Redesigned user experience for e-commerce luxury goods marketplace to promote credibility and trust in buyers and sellers.

Role

UX Designer

Date

09.2022 - 12.2022

Tools

Figma

Project

Website Redesign

Overview

Known Source, a UK-based startup based on creating a luxury goods e-commerce marketplace to tackle the problem to excess fashion.

My team was asked to redesign their website storefront to attract users before their launch.

Known Source needed help explaining their ecosystem of circular curation.

My Role

I was lead ux designer on a team of 2 stakeholders, 1 manager, and 3 designers. I was in charge of communicating with our stakeholders and manager with alignment.

Focus

  • About and Dealers Pages
  • Led the creation of the prototype
  • Consistency of Design
  • User Testing

Design Process

Challenge

In initial user tests, clients discovered:

  • Lack of understanding from users of what Known Source was about.
  • Difficulties navigating the site.
  • Had no connection to the brand or community.

Understanding Problems

The client provided us with user personas of what sorts of users they wanted to target.

Research Methodologies

  • Usability Testing (current site)
  • User interviews
  • Surveys

Key Insights

Merging user and business goals for the client, the design will reflect:

1

Create community

Copy text and features that create community on and off the platform.

2

Trust amongst users

Establish trust between buyers, dealers, and Known Source, through clarity of status of goods and processing.

3

Sustainability

Copy text supports message of sustainability through buying second hand.

Design

Curation through community

Explore known source through your favorite dealers

Outcomes

Successfully completed client presentation and design proposal.

  • Delivered prototypes from pre- and post- user testing.
  • Presentation was received well, and planned integration of the about page and later additions for other pages.

Postmortem

Our team presented the redesign in a presentation style to show case our research, process, and insights. The clients liked the presentation, after the review.

The clients decided to integrate the about page first as it strengthen their credibility and humanize the company.

Reflection

Since our client was based in the UK, it was hard to get feedback and initial insight to how their dealers and selling process looked like. Since we were in charge of the user facing side, I was not aware how the envisioned integrating the dealers page as well as I would have liked.
