Dall-e 2 Redesign

Case Study

Duration

2022.10 - 2022.11

Role

UX Designer

Tools

Figma

A redesign of the AI image generation platform, Dall-E 2’s website for sharing of AI images with others and profile personalization.

→ Prototype

My role

As UX designer and researcher, I collaborated with an advisor to recieve feedback on design decisions. I worked through the design process from initial research to prototypes and user testing.

Defintion

As personalization and identity is important when using AI-assisted technologies, users need to be able to assume collaboration with Dall-e 2. Correct contact information and ease of crediting users when sharing creations is essential to ai generated creations.

Before

After

Context

As a user of emerging technology, I found that when I initially signed up for Dall-e 2 I mispelled my name. I wanted to change my name and went to the settings page.

I discovered through user interviews, that others users had trouble finding their creations and understand how creations would be shared publically or private to the user.

Problem
Users cannot change their personal information.

Findings

Through looking at these competitors, I noticed that AI does not consider creating beginner friendly designs. How might someone who is not acustom to digital art or ai learn how to use these sites

User Testing

After user testing, users found that the visibility of a creation was unclear and they wanted to quickly change the status of visible or hidden creations.

User Test 1

After designing a prototype I conducted user testing with 5 users from the previous group to find that all users needed more information on disguishing between what they have publicly shared version kept private.

User Test 2

After redesigning those features, I tested with a new set of 5 users. User were able to more clearly understand the share feature.

Reflection

I presented my findings and proposed solution to my UX mentor. He gave me feedback through the process and liked the redesign.

Shortly after I finished my redesign, OpenAI created a filter system for their user’s creations. It seems that this was a successful solution as OpenAI also added the feature.
